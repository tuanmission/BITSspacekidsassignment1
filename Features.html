MVF 4 Visibility of System Status and Annoucement SYstem

Essential announcement is an efficient tool which can be considered as a link between the users and developer follows. The website needs to be able to inform clients about what is happening behind the scene (i.e. back end operation). This may be something related to service maintenance, application update, or connection glitches. The chief goal is to increase the users’ control and freedom. Since they will know what to expect in advance, better approach in scheduling other activities if, for example, there is an interval before the webpage can be accessed. This information can be displayed in numerous forms contingent on which types of situations and their severity. To illustrate, if the action is unreadable, the interrelated warning will pop up beneath the fraud (e.g. wrong input types). They might appear in red color if the data being conveyed is important, or alternatively some interpolation (e.g. underlining, bold words) can be used to support students with disabilities like color blindness. For other general announcements, these can occasionally emerge at the web header while pupils are engaging the website. Private messages such as personal awards, or data uniquely involved in the individual would better be transferred privately.

Most users want to achieve informative support and documentation in order to perform effectively, with minimum effort on recalling the process. This tendency is reversed for web programmers, since we want to inform website visitors with essential announcements, from that the usability and the error prevention are fortified.

MVF 5 Secure Storage of Data

As with all web applications that run on a client server framework, the data must be stored securely where login details are encrypted to prevent unauthorised access. When the user is logged in, the website must enter secure mode with SSL and HTTPS to prevent eavesdropping attacks which can compromise the security of the data, causing the sites reputation to be lost.

EVF 2 Ranking System

This behaves similar to a point calculator, fundamentally, students will get points by completing assigned tasks. These marks are accumulated in the range of 0 to 100 inclusively, in order to place the account in the corresponding rank being bronze, silver, gold respectively. The feature is presumably another social interaction of our project getting students to be recognised despite boundaries such as their background, health issues, physical limitations. The ranks are demonstrated through the profile picture of the user, to be more specific we are using the frame appearance (e.g. opulent shape and golden cover of the avatar). In some complicated questions, students occupying high points by solving these will have their account stick on a ‘fame board’. Additionally, some virtual events can be organised intermittently when users from different ranks can socialise, so this is great chances for them to learn from others. In addition, by the end of seasons, learners with high positions will be rewarded with specialties, by which they can enrich their treasure storage.

Students normally require a competitive environment to evolve themselves. Besides, it is common that individuals want to be recognised for their efforts, especially in the case of youngsters. Therefore, ranking implementation is a perfect match for these needs.

The ranks are considered successful when the competitive level is constrained properly, and negative tendencies are controlled effectively. As evidence, materialisation of the ranking system may occur (e.g. Users with experience receive payment from others to illegally improve the ranks on behalf of the payers). Besides, it is crucial not to discourage learners from lower ranks. Therefore, incentives and clear instructions should be suggested efficiently to tackle these problems.

EVF 3 Mentoring

Avoiding solely interaction between children and the digital website, supplementary supervision by adults is likely articulated. Mentors can also support students struggling with academic performance by giving hints, raising relevant questions, opening effective paths, etc. This session resides in documentation and help area, so subsequently instruct learners where to search for assistance. Beyond academic goals, children are also educated to ask for help from adults when they face impasse, vices in real life (e.g. bullying, or bad temptation)

Various methods of approaching the mentors are also available, namely video calls, messages, enquiry forms. Henceforth, students especially those experiencing impairments can select the most suitable way to describe their questions.

As learners or even parents, we want ourselves or our children to have access to high quality and delicate resources, so the best learning environment can be obtained. Or from teachers or mentors’ positions, we demand a productive workspace, so we can dedicate ourselves to teaching and straighten the younger generation.

Respect and appreciation are vital factors to validate this offer. Typically, questions about how professional are mentors? Are they patient and careful enough to assist learners with goal achievements? Or in contrast, whether students are polite and respectful while engaging the service. Therefore, the requirements of strict policies regarding attitude appraisal are necessary. Afterward, attention on the technical side is also indispensable for this operation, such as stable connection between end devices, sound and animation quality.
